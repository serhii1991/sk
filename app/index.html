ABOUT ME

Hello, my name is a Serhii. I’m Front-end developer.

I take great pride in my work, and I always try to better myself with every project I work on. I like to write semantic, cross browser compatible code by hand that is re-useable, maintainable and easy to understand.

I am motivated, enthusiastic and well organized, with a keen eye for beautiful design, primarily based around user interfaces. The main areas of my expertise are HTML(5), CSS(3) and JavaScript (native and jQuery) but I have minimal knowledge of PHP and MySQL.

I have worked with a number of high profile clients (both directly and indirectly) and I am comfortable working under pressure to complex specifications. I regularly use version control systems (GIT, Mercurial) to maintain my code, and I specialize in responsive web design (RWD). My preferred CMS is WordPress. I very like create a web sites, difficult thinks are not stop me.

Front-end is not my work, this my life.

Interested in working with me? Get in touch and I’d be happy to talk to you.
